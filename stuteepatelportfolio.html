The After Party

Accordion Fold Book
Image Series Project
2019
Using the red plastic cup as the main source of inspiration and subject for this project, I allude to the idea of an after party. I created different images using various medias to form this representations. The photos were developed using scanners, DSLR and Iphone cameras as well as Photoshop and vector drawings.

Portrait Series

Digital Photography 
2021
I dressed models, created sets and experimented with different styles of portraits to create a narrative about the subject. I enjoy playing with natural lighting as well as the human body itself.

From Kaccholi to Piscataway

Archive Book 
Hand binded 
2020
The archive I chose to present is the exchange of love letters between my mother and my father. The letters were written after their marriage, when my mother was living in the U.S., and my father was finishing up his studies in India.
