01


The After Party

Accordion Fold Book
Image Series Project
2019
Using the red plastic cup as the main source of inspiration and subject for this project, I allude to the idea of an after party. I created different images using various medias to form this representations. The photos were developed using scanners, DSLR and Iphone cameras as well as Photoshop and vector drawings.

02


Portrait Series

Digital Photography 
2021
I dressed models, created sets and experimented with different styles of portraits to create a narrative about the subject. I enjoy playing with natural lighting as well as the human body itself.

03

 Regrowth

UI/UX Design
App Design
2020
Regrowth is a sustainability app that recommends shops to users based on current stores that they shop at. It also helps users find specific items that they are in search of. The app only provides the names of certified companies that have been confirmed in using environmentally socially and ethical practices.

04


From Kaccholi to Piscataway

Archive Book 
Hand binded 
2020
The archive collection I chose to present is the exchange of love letters between my mother and my father. The letters were written after their marriage, when my mother was living in the U.S., and my father was finishing up his studies in India.

07


Evergreen Lecture Series

Zoom Lecture Series Posters
2021
These posters were created for a zoom lecture series entitled “Evergreen Lecture Series” based on the artist lectures hosted by Evergreen State College. Multiple speakers discussed different topics, some of which included studying feminist utopianism, synthesizing external and internal experiences and examining the collapse of the climate.

08


Vedic Astrology
HTML and CSS website
2020
click click here to view site
Astrology is the study of the plants, stars, moons and their alignments. The western world studies astrology completely differently than the eastern world does. Given that the differentiation is not widely known, I created an interactive website to not only exhibit their differences, but also to display information about Vedic Astrology in a more visual way.

Climate Change

Protect Our Home
After Effects Animation
2020
For the “Rewriting Histories” Mason Gross exhibition, I created an animation to raise awareness for climate change. Our planet is only getting warmer resulting in major environmental changes. We only have one Earth, and we have to do better in protecting it.

Red Cup

Plastic Cup Series
2019
I initially created imagery using the red, plastic cup as my subject. With these photos, I then developed posters that reflect the connotations commonly associated with the object like plastic waste and drunk driving.
